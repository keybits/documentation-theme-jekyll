- Very fast results - see [this example on VueJS docs](http://vuejs.org/v2/guide/)
- Categorised results to make clear which section docs are in (see above example)
- Excellent [analytics and stats](https://www.algolia.com/doc/guides/analytics/analytics-overview/) on what people search for
- They have a [Jekyll plugin](https://github.com/algolia/algoliasearch-jekyll) that makes getting good search results customizable and straightoforard
